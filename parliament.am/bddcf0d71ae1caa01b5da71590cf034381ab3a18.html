Born on January 23, 1986 in Yerevan.

2007 - 2009 - Served in the Armed Forces of the Republic of Armenia.

2003 - Entered the Computer Systems and Computer Science Department of the Armenian State Engineering University.

2005 - Transferred, 2007 - Graduated from the SAED (Synopsys Armenia Educational Department).

2011 - Graduated with a Master's Degree from the “Synopsys Armenia” Educational Department, receiving specialty of Automation of Electronic Design.

February 1, 2007 - June 15, 2007 - Junior programmer of OJSC “Armproject.”

July 1, 2009 - September 30, 2009 - Programmer of OJSC “Armproject.”

October 1, 2009 - May 31, 2010 - Administrator of the site of the “Stream Tech” LLC.

June 1, 2010 - August 30, 2010 - Probation period in the “NairiSoft” company.

December 1, 2010 - December 30, 2014 - Web programmer of the company “Altakod.”

January 12, 2015 - January 31, 2017 - Head of the web department of the English company “IAM Cloud.”

2013 - 2014 - Member of the coordinating group of the “I am against” initiative.

2015 - Member of the coordinating group of the “Bright Armenia” initiative.

2015 - Elected a member of the council of the “Bright Armenia” party.

April 2, 2017 - Elected Member of the National Assembly from the territorial electoral list of the electoral district #2 of the alliance of parties “Way Out.”

Married, with three children.
