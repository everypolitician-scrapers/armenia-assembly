Born on September 23, 1967 in Yerevan.

1987 - Graduated from the Painting Faculty of the Panos Terlemezyan State College of Fine Arts in Yerevan. Teacher of painting and graphic design.

1993 - Graduated from the Graphic Department of the Yerevan State Academy of Fine Arts. Painter and graphic artist. Honoured Artist of the Republic of Armenia.

1989 - 1991 - Costume and stage designer in the “Ardzaganq” Theatre - Studio (Metro Theatre). Designed three performances - “The Mythical Eternity,” “Antigone,” “the Master and the Servant.”

1994 - 1997 - Worked in State Theatre of Song.

Since 1996 - The soloist of Armenian State Philharmonic Orchestra.

2002 - 2003 - Lecturer of the Yerevan State Institute of Theater and Film.

2012 - 2017 - Member of the National Assembly (proportional electoral system, RPA). Member of the Standing Committee on Science, Education, Culture, Youth and Sport of the National Assembly. Member of the “Republican” (RPA) faction. Member of the RPA.

April 2, 2017 - Elected Member of the National Assembly from the RPA national electoral list.

2008 - Awarded the Gold Medal by the Mayor of Yerevan.

2008 - Awarded the “Vazgen Sargsyan” Order by the Ministry of Defense of the Republic of Armenia.

2014 - Awarded the Medal “Gratitude” (NKR).

Awarded a number of musical awards:

1992 - “The Best Singer” (RA).

1993 - The Grand Prize (International Festival “Charm Show.”)

1996 - “The Best Singer of the Year” (award-giving ceremony “Asup,” RA).

2000 - “The Armenian Star of the 20th Century” (Festival “Star,” RA).

2000, 2001, 2004, 2006 - The Best Female Performer (award-giving ceremony “Anush,” USA).

2001, 2003, 2004 - “Golden Lyre” (RA).

2000, 2004 - “The Best Voice of the Independent Armenia” (award-giving ceremony “Krunk”).

2009 - “Diva” (Annual Song Festival, USA).

2013 - “The Best Artist of the 10th anniversary” (award-giving ceremony “Armenia” RF).

2013 - Honourable citizen of NKR Martakert.

2005 - Academician of the International Academy of Sciences of Nature and Society.

Married, with two children.
