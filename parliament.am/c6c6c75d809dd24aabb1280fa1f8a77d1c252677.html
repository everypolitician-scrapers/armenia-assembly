Born on July 1, 1962 in Yerevan.

1981 - Graduated from the piano division of R. Melikian Music College.

1988 - Studied in Actor’s course of Yerevan State Artistic - Theatrical Institute.

1992 - Armenian - British Business - School, received businessperson - manager qualification.

1999 - Received high qualification of bank employee in the strategy planning area of trade banks, (IBTI, Vienna).

1982 - 1984 - Music teacher and concertmaster at St. Jrbashian #8 music school.

1988 - Director, senior research worker at scientific - methodological center of the Ministry of Culture of ASSR.

1992 - Director of the General editorial Staff of the Drama Programmes of “Armenian National Television”.

1994 - 1996 - Director of “Metro” Theatre.

1996 - 1998 - Director of “Navak - 1,” a daughter enterprise of the Chess All-Armenian Charitable “Navak” fund.

1998 - Counselor to the General Director, later head of Staff and Secretariat in “ArmSavingsBank.”

2000 - 2002 - Adviser of the Director of the Armenian Center of Strategic and National Researches.

2002 - 2004 - Head of financial division in “GTMC” (General TransWorld manufacturing Company) CJSC.

2004 - 2005 - Chief Counselor to the Director of “Kanaker” CJSC.

2005 - 2009 - General Director in “Arvest” (Art) fund.

2008 - Counselor to the Minister of Culture of the Republic of Armenia.

2009 - February - May - Director at “Choreography State Theatre,” a Non State - Commercial Organization.

2010 - Member of the Management Council of Yerevan Institute of Theatre and Cinema.

2012 - Member of the Management Council of Yerevan Brusov State Linguistic University.

Since 2005 - Member of the Women’s Council of the Republican Party of Armenia, Head of Cultural Committee.

May 12, 2009 - May 31, 2012 - Deputy of the National Assembly (proportional electoral system, RPA). Member of the Standing Committee on Science, Education, Culture, Youth and Sport of the National Assembly. Member of the RPA faction. Member of the RPA Council.

2012 - 2017 - Elected Deputy of the National Assembly by the proportional electoral system from the Republican Party of Armenia. Member of the Standing Committee on Science, Education, Culture, Youth and Sport of the National Assembly. Member of the “Republican” (RPA) faction.

April 2, 2017 - Elected Deputy of the National Assembly by the RPA national electoral list.

2016 - Awarded the Medal of “Garegin Nzhdeh” of the Ministry of Defense of the Republic of Armenia.

Married, with a child.
