Born on May 30, 1948.

1972 - Graduated from the Mechanical engineering Faculty of the K. Marx Yerevan Polytechnic Institute.

1972 - Worked at the ASSR NAS Byurakan optical-mechanical laboratory.

1977 - Worked at Special Radio-Physics and Electronics Design Office.

1983 - Head of   industrial - dispatching department  “Armavto.”

1983 - 1986  - Director of Ashtarak Knitted - goods Factory.

1986 - 1987 - Director of Charentsavan clothes factory.

1987 - 1990 - Chair of the Charentsavan City Council.

1996 - 1998  - Chief Advisor to the Prime Minister of the Republic of Armenia.

1997 - 2000 - International Expert on UN issues of decentralization and civil service.

1997 - Head of Working Group for Developing the Concept and the Law on Civil Service in the Republic of Armenia.

Since 1997 - Teaching at the State Academy of Public Administration of the Republic of Armenia.

1999 - 2000 - Deputy Prime Minister of the Republic of Armenia - Minister of Territorial Administration of the Republic of Armenia.

2000 - 2002 - Advisor to the President of the Republic of Armenia, Vice - Chair of the State Commission on the organization of the festivities related to the 1700 anniversary of the proclamation of Christianity as state religion in Armenia, the main coordinator of activities.

2009 - Member of the Public Council, adjunct to the President of the Republic of Armenia.

Since 2003 - Presides over the Christian - Democratic Union in Armenia.

1990 - 1992 - Member of the Supreme Council of the Republic of Armenia. (electoral district #144.) Member and later Chair of the Standing Committee on Local Self – Government Affairs of the Supreme Council of the Republic of Armenia.

1992  - Member of the Armenian delegation in OSCE. Member of the Armenian delegation in OSCE Minsk Group on settlement of the Nagorno-Karabakh conflict.

August 1992 - February 1993  - Prime  Minister of the Republic of Armenia.

1993 - 1995 - Member of the Supreme Council (electoral district #36.) Secretary of the Standing Committee on Financial - Credit and Budgetary Affairs of the Supreme Council.

1995 - 1999 - Member of the National Assembly of the Republic of Armenia (electoral district #7.) Member of the Standing Committee on State and Legal Affairs of the National Assembly.

Not included in any factions and deputy groups.

1998 - 1999 - President of the National Assembly of the Republic of Armenia.

Author of the initiative of March 16, 1999 Strasbourg Memorandum on Parliamentary Cooperation of the South Caucasian Republics under the auspices of the Parliamentary Assembly of the Council of Europe.

May 6, 2012 - Elected Deputy of the National Assembly by the proportional electoral system from the Republican Party of Armenia.

2002 - Awarded Medal of “Movses Khorenatsi."

Author of dozens of articles.

Married, with two children.
