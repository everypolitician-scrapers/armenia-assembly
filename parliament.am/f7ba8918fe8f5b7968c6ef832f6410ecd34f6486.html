Born on April 8, 1948 in Yerevan.

1971 - Graduated from the Philological Faculty of the Yerevan State University. Philologist.

1970 - 1972 - Metalworker in the Yerevan Silk Complex.

1972 - 1991 - Teacher at School #30, the Deputy Headmas¬ter at Schools #94 and #153, Headmaster at Schools #3 and #185.

1991 - 1996 - Deputy Chairman and later First Deputy Chairman of the Executive Committee of Mashtots District Council in Yerevan.

1995 - 1999 - Member of the National Assembly (electoral district #32). Member of the Standing Committee on Science, Education, Culture and Youth Affairs of the National Assembly. Member of the “Republic” faction, then member of the “Yerkrapah” deputy group.

1999 - 2003 - Member of the National Assembly (proportional electoral system, “Unity” alliance). Vice Chair of the Standing Committee on Science, Education, Culture and Youth Affairs of the National Assembly. Member of the “Unity” faction, Head of the “Unity” faction.

2003 - 2007 - Member of the National Assembly (proportional electoral system, RPA). Member of the Standing Committee on State and Legal Affairs of the National Assembly. Head of the RPA faction.

2007 - 2012 - Member of the National Assembly (proportional electoral system, RPA).

June 19, 2007 - November 17, 2008 - Member of the Standing Committee on Defense, National Security and Internal Affairs of the National Assembly.

November 11, 2008 - May 31, 2012 - Head of the RPA faction. RPA Deputy Head, member of the RPA Executive Body and Council.

2012 - 2017 - Member of the National Assembly (proportional electoral system, RPA).

May 31, 2012 - May 7, 2014 - Head of the RPA faction, RPA Deputy Head, member of the RPA Executive Body and Council.

April 29, 2014 - May 5, 2017 - President of the National Assembly of the Republic of Armenia.

April 2, 2017 - Elected Member of the National Assembly from the RPA national electoral list.

2004 - Awarded the Medal of Honour of the National Assembly of the Republic of Armenia.

2005 - Awarded the Medal “Vazgen Sargsyan.”

2006 - Awarded the RA Prime Minister’s Commemorative Medal.

2006 - Awarded the Commemorative Medal of the Ministry of Education and Science of the Republic of Armenia.

2007 - Awarded the Commemorative Medal “Honourable Member of the Educational Centre” of the P. Yavorov School #131.

2008 - Awarded the “Mesrop Mashtots” Medal.

2008 - Awarded the “Eagle of Mountainous Armenia” Golden Commemorative Medal.

2008 - Awarded the “Khachatur Abovian” Medal.

2010 - Awarded the “Fridtjof Nansen” Commemorative Gold Medal.

2011 - Awarded the “Mkhitar Gosh” Medal.

2012 - Awarded the 90th Anniversary Armenian State Pedagogical University Medal.

2013 - Awarded the "Garegin Nzhdeh'' Medal of the Ministry of Defense of the Republic of Armenia.

2014 - Awarded the “Armenian Eagle” Commemorative Gold Medal.

2014 - Awarded the “YVU 20 Years” Jubilee Medal of the Republic of Armenia.

2015 - Awarded the YVU “Sparapet Vazgen Sargsyan” Order.

2015 - Awarded the “Cooperation” Order of CIS Inter - Parliamentary Assembly. 2015 - Awarded the Medal of “Federal Council 20th Anniversary” of the FC Federal Assembly of the Russian Federation.

2016 - Awarded the first rank Order “For Services Contributed to Motherland.”

2017 - Awarded the “Mesrop Mashtots” Order (NKR).

2017 - Awarded the Medal of “CIS IPA 25 Years.”

Married, with two children.
